Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Markdown Editor Extension

This Chrome extension ("Markdown Editor") is designed to help users write and preview Markdown content.

Data Collection

This extension does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage to:

  • Auto-save your Markdown content on your device
  • Restore your work when you reopen the editor
  • Store your language and theme preferences

All data stays 100% on your device.

Permissions

  • storage: Saves your work locally (no cloud upload)
  • clipboardWrite: Copies content only when you click copy buttons

Third-Party Services

This extension does NOT use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.

Summary: Your data stays on your device. We don't see, collect, or store any of your content.
